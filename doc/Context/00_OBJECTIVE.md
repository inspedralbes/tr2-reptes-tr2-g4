# Project Objective

The primary objective of the **Plataforma Intercentre de Traspàs d'Adaptacions Educatives amb IA** is to bridge the communication gap between different educational institutions (ESO and FP) regarding student pedagogical adaptations.

## Core Goals
1.  **Standardization:** Use AI to transform heterogeneous documents (PDF, Word, XML) into a structured format.
2.  **Efficiency:** Automate the summarization of Individualized Plans (PI) to provide teachers with actionable insights.
3.  **Security:** Ensure strict compliance with GDPR through data anonymization and secure transfer protocols.
4.  **Continuity:** Guarantee that every student's educational needs are met immediately upon enrollment in a new center.
